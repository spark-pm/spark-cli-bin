SPARK is a lightweight project management framework designed for high-velocity teams of 1-30 people. The CLI provides:

  • Workspace Management - Initialize and manage team workspaces
  • Task Tracking - Kanban-style task management with pull-based workflow
  • OKR Management - Quarterly objectives and key results tracking
  • Team Collaboration - Multi-user workspaces with real-time sync
  • AI-Agent Friendly - Designed for integration with AI development workflows
